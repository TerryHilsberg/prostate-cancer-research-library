# Category Index

Use these categories to file studies, guideline updates, patient decision aids, and clinical questions.

| Category | What belongs here | Patient-facing question |
|---|---|---|
| Screening & Early Detection | PSA, MRI-first pathways, risk calculators, hormone/risk biomarkers, screening intervals, overdiagnosis | Should I be tested, retested, or monitored? |
| Diagnostic Workup | Biopsy approaches, MRI interpretation, pathology, infection/bleeding risk | How reliable is the diagnosis? |
| Risk Stratification & Staging | Gleason/Grade Group, PSA density, TNM, nomograms, genomic classifiers | How aggressive is it? |
| Active Surveillance | Eligibility, monitoring schedules, triggers for treatment, long-term outcomes | Can treatment safely wait? |
| Therapies | Systemic therapy, immunotherapy, immune modulation, anti-inflammatory strategies, treatment sequencing, investigational approaches | What therapies are standard, biomarker-selected, or experimental? |
| Localized Treatment | Surgery, radiation modalities, focal therapy, comparative outcomes | What are the curative options and trade-offs? |
| Advanced & Recurrent Disease | ADT, AR pathway inhibitors, chemotherapy, radioligand therapy, recurrence | What if it has spread or returns? |
| Imaging & Biomarkers | mpMRI, PSMA PET, liquid/tissue biomarkers, hormone markers, genomic tests | What extra tests might clarify risk? |
| Side Effects & Quality of Life | Urinary, sexual, bowel, fatigue, mental health, survivorship | What will life be like after treatment? |
| Nutrition | Diet quality, body composition, metabolic health, supplements, nutrition during treatment and survivorship | What nutrition evidence is relevant, and what should I discuss with a clinician or dietitian? |
| Exercise | Resistance training, aerobic fitness, fatigue, function, bone/muscle health, exercise during treatment and survivorship | What activity is safe and useful in my situation? |
| Shared Decision-Making | Decision aids, patient preferences, communication, second opinions | How do I choose between reasonable options? |
| Guidelines & Care Pathways | NCCN/EAU/AUA/ASCO/NICE updates, multidisciplinary pathways | What do authoritative guidelines say? |

## Filing metadata

Each filed item should include:

- Citation and source URL/DOI/PMID
- Publication date and retrieval date
- Evidence type and population
- Category or categories
- Patient relevance
- Main finding
- Limitations and uncertainty
- Clinical questions raised
