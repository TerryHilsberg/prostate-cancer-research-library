# Therapies

## Scope

Research notes and filed articles for **therapies** across localized, recurrent, and advanced prostate cancer. This category is for treatment concepts that cut across disease stage or treatment modality, including systemic therapy, immunotherapy, immune modulation, anti-inflammatory strategies, treatment sequencing, and investigational approaches.

## Key patient questions

- What is standard-of-care, what is biomarker-selected, and what is investigational?
- Which disease state or risk group does the evidence apply to?
- Does the therapy improve overall survival, progression-free survival, symptoms, quality of life, or only a surrogate marker such as PSA?
- What are the risks, interactions, and uncertainties?
- What should be discussed with a qualified clinician before starting, stopping, or changing treatment?
