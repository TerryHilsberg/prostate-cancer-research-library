# Questions for Clinicians

## Diagnosis and risk

- What is the most likely explanation for my PSA/test result?
- Should PSA be repeated before further testing?
- Would mpMRI help decide whether biopsy is needed?
- If biopsy is recommended, why this approach and what are the risks?
- If cancer is found, what Grade Group, stage, and risk category is it?

## Treatment choice

- Is active surveillance reasonable for this risk profile?
- What are the expected outcomes with surgery vs radiation vs surveillance?
- What side effects are most likely for someone in my situation?
- How many similar cases do you manage per year?
- Should I get a second opinion or multidisciplinary review?

## Follow-up

- What result would trigger a change in plan?
- How often would PSA, MRI, biopsy, or imaging be repeated?
- How will quality-of-life side effects be monitored and treated?
