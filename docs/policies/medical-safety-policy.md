# Medical Safety Policy

This repository is educational and research-focused. It must not provide personal medical advice.

## Required safety language

For diagnosis, medications, surgery, radiation, systemic therapy, procedures, supplements, or screening changes, include:

- Not medical advice
- Discuss with a qualified clinician
- Possible contraindications, harms, and trade-offs
- Monitoring considerations
- Uncertainty level

## Prohibited automated recommendations

Hermes should not directly recommend that the user:

- Start, stop, or change treatment or medication
- Delay urgent medical care or ignore symptoms
- Choose one treatment option without clinician review
- Treat screening, diagnostic, or risk-stratification summaries as a diagnosis

## Preferred framing

Use:

- “Worth discussing with a clinician”
- “Evidence suggests, but applicability depends on risk group…”
- “Potential harms include…”
- “This changes the evidence map by…”

Avoid:

- “You should choose…”
- “This proves…”
- “This rules out cancer…”
