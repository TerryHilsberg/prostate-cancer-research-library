    Paper Repository

    This section can hold deeper notes on individual papers or curated paper collections.

    Curated collections

    • Exercise and Prostate Cancer: Evidence Map — best available evidence on exercise/physical activity before diagnosis, after diagnosis, during treatment, and after treatment/survivorship.
    • Nutrition and Prostate Cancer: Evidence Map — best available evidence on diet, dietary patterns, metabolic health, fasting mimicking diet/protein-IGF-mTOR questions, and selected supplements before diagnosis, after diagnosis, during treatment, and after treatment/survivorship.
    • Blood Pressure, Hypertension, and Prostate Cancer: Evidence Map — literature review on blood pressure/hypertension, prostate cancer incidence/fatal risk, recurrence uncertainty, and treatment-related hypertension/cardiovascular risk.
    • STIs, UTIs, Prostatitis, and PSA Levels: Evidence Map — evidence on how urinary tract infections, sexually transmitted infections, prostatitis, and systemic infections can affect PSA interpretation and follow-up decisions.
    • Transperineal vs Transrectal Prostate Biopsy: Evidence Map — literature review comparing biopsy routes, infection risk, cancer detection, pain/tolerability, circumstances where each is used, and trends over time.
    • Focal Therapy for Localized Prostate Cancer: Evidence Map — review of focal and partial-gland treatment options including HIFU, cryotherapy, irreversible electroporation, focal laser ablation, vascular-targeted photodynamic therapy, TULSA/MR-guided ultrasound, focal brachytherapy, and focal radiation boost; includes patient-selection logic and validation evidence.
    • PSMA PET/CT in Prostate Cancer: Evidence Map — evidence on PSMA PET/CT for diagnosis-adjacent use, high-risk staging, biochemical recurrence, radioligand-therapy selection, and treatment-monitoring questions.
    • Immune Modulation and Anti-inflammatory Agents in Prostate Cancer: Evidence Map — state-of-the-art review of approved, biomarker-selected, investigational, and hypothesis-generating immune/anti-inflammatory strategies.
    • Hormone Levels and Prostate Cancer Incidence: Evidence Map — evidence on testosterone, free testosterone, SHBG, LH, IGF-I, and prostate cancer incidence/aggressive-disease risk, centered on the 2026 Lancet Healthy Longevity IPD meta-analysis.
    • Metabolic Therapy as an Adjunct in Prostate Cancer: Evidence Review and Critique — combined take on a Gemini analysis of Seyfried-style metabolic therapy, ketogenic/low-carbohydrate diets, calorie restriction, insulin/IGF signaling, and prostate-cancer evidence gaps.
    • Genetic and Epigenetic Background to Prostate Cancer Risk: Evidence Map — inherited variants, polygenic risk, tumour genetics, epigenetic biomarkers, predicted incidence, unknowns, and promising research directions prompted by the 2026 UBC/CDK12 hereditary prostate-cancer report.
